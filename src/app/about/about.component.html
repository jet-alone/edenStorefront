About Me

Hi! I'm Tyler Shelton. Ever since I was a young boy, I would visit my grandpappy in the Netherlands. He was a well-known gardener around his village in those parts, toiling away at all hours as he curated his beautiful gardens. I learned a lot under his tutelage, as well as from the garden gnomes who would visit me in the nighttime and whisper gardening secrets into my ears. Both my talented grandpappy and the Netherland garden gnomes taught me everything I know about the craft. As such, I wanted to bring my wonderful garden products to the people of the world. On Eden.com you will find many plant products, from vegetables to home potted plants to trees of all kinds. Please browse for whatever product that interests you and feel free to bask in the beauty of gardening. From my backyard...to yours.
